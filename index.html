What It Does

A receptionist that never clocks out

From the first ring to a confirmed consultation — your AI Receptionist handles the full client intake cycle, 24 hours a day.

Answers Every Call

Picks up instantly — no hold music, no voicemail. Every client intake call is handled professionally from the first ring, even at 11 PM on a Friday.

Books Consultations

Checks your real-time availability and books initial consultations directly — Google Calendar, Outlook, or your practice management system.

Handles Common Questions

Trained on your firm — practice areas, initial steps, general fee structures, office hours. Answers confidently so your team don't have to field routine calls.

Routes Complex Calls

Knows when a caller needs a solicitor directly. Transfers with full context — the matter type, caller details, and what's been said — so nothing is lost in handover.

Sends Confirmations

Automatically sends the caller a confirmation via SMS or email with their consultation time and any preparation notes your firm specifies.

Works Around the Clock

Nights, weekends, bank holidays. Prospective clients who call after hours are captured and booked — not lost to a competitor who happened to pick up.

Pricing

Simple, transparent pricing

No opaque contracts, no hidden costs. Two ways to run it — pick what fits your firm.

One-time setup fee. Covers the full build: custom AI voice, calendar integration, call routing logic, and training on your firm's practice areas, FAQs, and client intake process. Quoted after your discovery call based on complexity — most firms are live within a week of that conversation.

Option A

Pay-as-you-go

Pay per call handled. No monthly commitment, no minimum volume. Scale up or down as call volume changes. A good fit for smaller practices or firms wanting to trial the system before committing.

Option B

Monthly subscription

Fixed monthly fee, unlimited calls handled. Predictable overhead, complete coverage. Recommended for firms with consistent or high call volumes — typically more cost-effective from around 80+ calls per month.
