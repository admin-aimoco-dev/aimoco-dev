What It Does

A receptionist that never clocks out

Your AI receptionist handles the full cycle — from answering the call to confirming the booking in your calendar.

Answers Every Call

Picks up instantly — no hold music, no voicemail. Your callers speak to a natural-sounding AI that understands their request.

Books Into Your Calendar

Checks your real-time availability and books appointments directly — Google Calendar, Outlook, or any system you use.

Handles Common Questions

Trained on your business — opening hours, services, pricing, directions. Answers confidently without needing a human.

Routes Complex Calls

Knows when a caller needs a real person. Transfers to your team with full context — nothing lost in translation.

Sends Confirmations

Automatically sends your caller a confirmation via SMS or email with the appointment details. No manual follow-up.

Works Around the Clock

Nights, weekends, holidays. Every call answered, every appointment captured — even when your office is closed.
